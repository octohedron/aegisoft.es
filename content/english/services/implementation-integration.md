Deliver IAM implementations that are production-safe, measurable, and maintainable across enterprise complexity.

> **TL;DR recommendation:** Use this service when you need to deliver IAM migration or rollout with controlled risk. The first phase usually produces a readiness assessment, dependency map, wave plan, and rollback strategy.

## Who this is for

- IAM platform owners and engineering leaders
- Teams migrating or consolidating identity providers
- Organizations with large application portfolios and strict uptime requirements

## When to call us

- You need to migrate from legacy IAM with minimal disruption
- Integration debt is slowing SSO/MFA rollout
- Policy inconsistencies are creating security and support risk
- You need an implementation partner with deep protocol expertise

## What you get in the first engagement

- Implementation readiness assessment and dependency map
- Platform architecture and rollout wave plan
- Risk register with mitigation and rollback strategy
- Delivery governance model and handover expectations

## Typical timeframe

- **Readiness and planning:** 2-3 weeks
- **Pilot wave:** 2-6 weeks
- **Phased production rollout:** 8-24 weeks

## Engagement models

- Technical assessment
- Implementation delivery
- Migration program advisory
- Managed stabilization support

Yes. Pilot-first rollout is recommended for high-risk or high-complexity environments.
