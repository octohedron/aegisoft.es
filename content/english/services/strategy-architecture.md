Design an IAM strategy that can pass enterprise scrutiny: clear target-state architecture, practical migration sequencing, and measurable risk reduction.

> **TL;DR recommendation:** Use this service when your organization needs a decision-grade IAM target architecture and roadmap before implementation. The first phase typically delivers a current-state baseline, target-state options, and a phased plan with risks, constraints, and measurable outcomes.

## Who this is for

- CTO, CISO, IAM lead, and enterprise architecture teams
- Organizations modernizing fragmented IAM estates
- Regulated environments requiring audit-ready architecture decisions

## When to call us

- You are planning an IAM platform migration or consolidation
- Audit findings expose access governance weaknesses
- Legacy identity architecture blocks cloud and product initiatives
- You need an executive-ready roadmap tied to delivery realities

## What you get in the first engagement

- IAM maturity assessment and current-state architecture baseline
- Gap analysis against business, security, and compliance objectives
- Target-state architecture covering authentication, federation, and lifecycle controls
- Phased roadmap with dependencies, risks, and resource assumptions
