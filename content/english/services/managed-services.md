> **TL;DR recommendation:** Use this service when your IAM environment is business-critical and your internal team needs operational reinforcement. The first phase defines support model, service levels, monitoring scope, and transition priorities.

- Teams running business-critical IAM environments
- Organizations lacking dedicated in-house IAM operations capacity
- Programs needing stronger uptime, incident response, and governance

## When to call us

- Recurring IAM incidents are impacting users or audit posture
- Internal teams are overloaded with manual IAM operations
- You need 24/7 monitoring without building a full IAM operations team
- Platform upgrades and policy changes are risky or delayed

- Operations baseline and service transition plan

- Monitoring and reporting framework
- Recommended service level and delivery model
