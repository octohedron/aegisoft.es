Use this checklist to run a fast first-pass compliance review with your security and IAM teams.

## Checklist scope

- Identity data minimization and retention controls
- MFA and adaptive authentication enforcement
- Access review cadence and evidence quality
- IAM incident detection, triage, and reporting
- Third-party IAM provider controls and assurance
- Operational resilience patterns (availability, failover, recovery)

## How to use it

1. Score each control area as green/amber/red.
2. Capture evidence sources for each score.
3. Prioritize red controls by business impact and audit exposure.
4. Build a phased remediation plan with owners and deadlines.
