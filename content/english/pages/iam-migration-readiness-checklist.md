This checklist helps engineering and IAM teams de-risk migration programs before execution.

## Readiness checks

- Application inventory and protocol dependencies (OIDC/SAML/LDAP)
- Tenant and environment design (dev/test/prod)
- Identity data model mapping and attribute normalization
- MFA policy parity and step-up authentication behavior
- Rollback, cutover, and incident-response planning
- Monitoring, logging, and post-go-live support readiness

## Output you should produce

- Migration wave plan by business criticality
- Risk register with mitigation owners
- Validation criteria for pilot and full production rollout
- Post-migration operations model
