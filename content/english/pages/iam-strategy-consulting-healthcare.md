## Healthcare IAM strategy

Strengthen identity controls while preserving clinical workflow speed and reliability.

## Typical priorities

- Secure access for clinical and administrative users
- SSO and MFA rollout across mixed legacy and modern systems
- Lifecycle and provisioning automation
- Audit-ready logging and access governance

## First engagement outputs

- Access model and risk profile by user type
- Integration and migration roadmap
- Delivery plan with uptime-safe deployment waves
