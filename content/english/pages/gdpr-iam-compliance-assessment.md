## GDPR-focused IAM assessment

We assess whether your IAM design and operations support GDPR obligations while maintaining secure user access.

## What we assess

- Identity data minimization and attribute governance
- Access lifecycle controls and review evidence
- Authentication, logging, and data protection controls
- Third-party and processor IAM control boundaries

## What you receive

- Current-state findings and risk ratings
- Prioritized remediation roadmap
- Practical control recommendations aligned to your operating model

## Suitable engagement paths

- One-time assessment
- Assessment + remediation implementation
- Continuous advisory for ongoing compliance
