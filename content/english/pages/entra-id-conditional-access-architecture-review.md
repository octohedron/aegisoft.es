## Conditional access architecture review

We evaluate policy design, risk controls, and operational behavior to help teams enforce secure access without excessive user friction.

## Review focus

- Policy structure and assignment strategy
- MFA and step-up rule effectiveness
- Break-glass, exception, and emergency access controls
- Logging, monitoring, and incident response readiness
- Alignment with compliance and audit evidence requirements

## Deliverables

- Policy risk and overlap findings
- Recommended architecture and governance model
- Prioritized implementation roadmap
