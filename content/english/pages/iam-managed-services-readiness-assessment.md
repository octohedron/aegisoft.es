## Scope

Assessment of IAM operations maturity and readiness for managed services transition.

## Inputs required

- Current support model and incident trends
- Platform operations documentation
- SLA targets and business constraints

## Deliverables

- Operating-model readiness score
- Gap analysis for tooling, process, and staffing
- Transition roadmap with service-level recommendations

## Timeline band

2-4 weeks

## Ideal customer profile

Organizations with growing IAM operational complexity and limited in-house specialist capacity.

## What happens after delivery

Transition plan for managed or hybrid IAM operations with SLA and governance structure.
