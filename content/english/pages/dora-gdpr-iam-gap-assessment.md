Assessment of IAM controls against DORA/GDPR requirements, with practical remediation sequencing.

## Inputs required

- Existing IAM architecture and policies
- Control documentation and recent audit findings
- Priority systems and risk owners

## Deliverables

- Control gap matrix (DORA/GDPR mapped to IAM controls)
- Risk-prioritized remediation plan
- Evidence strategy for audit-readiness

## Timeline band

2-4 weeks

## Ideal customer profile

Regulated enterprises preparing for compliance deadlines, internal remediation, or external audit review.
