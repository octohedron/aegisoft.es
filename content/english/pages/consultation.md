Select the path that matches your current priority. We reply within **1 business day**, and your request is triaged by an IAM architect.

## 1) High-intent: Book an IAM architecture consultation (30 min)

Best for teams already planning delivery and needing expert validation.

- **Who this is for:** CTO, CISO, IAM lead, enterprise architect
- **What you get:** focused architecture review, risk highlights, recommended next-step plan
- **Typical output:** short summary with key decisions and execution options

[Book architecture consultation](/contact)

## 2) Problem-based: Get the DORA/GDPR IAM gap checklist

Best for compliance-led programs preparing for audits, remediation, or board reporting.

- **Who this is for:** compliance leaders, security architects, risk managers
- **What you get:** practical checklist + suggested remediation priorities
- **Typical output:** initial control-gap map and recommended engagement scope

[Get DORA/GDPR IAM gap checklist](/resources/dora-gdpr-iam-gap-checklist)

## 3) Technical evaluation: Request IAM migration readiness review

Best for platform migration planning (Okta/Auth0/Keycloak/Entra).

- **Who this is for:** engineering leads and platform owners
- **What you get:** dependency map, migration risks, phased readiness plan
- **Typical output:** execution readiness scorecard and sequencing guidance

[Request migration readiness review](/resources/iam-migration-readiness-checklist)

## What happens next

1. We review your context and timeline.
2. An IAM architect responds with the best-fit path.
3. We run a short triage call and define scope.
4. You receive a clear proposal with milestones and deliverables.
