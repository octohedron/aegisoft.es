## Scope

Technical and delivery readiness review for IAM platform migration or consolidation.

## Inputs required

- Current and target IAM platform context
- Integration and dependency inventory
- Security and compliance requirements
- Timeline constraints

## Deliverables

- Migration dependency map
- Risk register and rollback strategy
- Suggested wave plan and execution gating

## Timeline band

2-3 weeks

## Ideal customer profile

Organizations migrating from or between IAM platforms with mixed legacy and modern app portfolios.

## What happens after delivery

You get a practical migration playbook and optional execution support model.
