## Methodology overview

Our IAM security and compliance engagements follow a structured model:

1. Assessment and control inventory
2. Gap analysis and risk prioritization
3. Remediation design and implementation planning
4. Validation and evidence alignment
5. Continuous monitoring and reporting

## Standard artifacts

- IAM control map by framework and regulatory requirement
- Architecture decision records
- Risk register and remediation tracker
- Audit evidence pack templates
- Operational runbooks and ownership model
