## Okta to Auth0 migration consulting

Migrate identity workloads from Okta to Auth0 with controlled rollout and minimal business disruption.

## Common migration challenges

- Policy parity across applications and tenants
- Legacy SAML/OIDC integration behavior differences
- User migration and identity data consistency
- Rollback and cutover risk under production constraints

## First engagement deliverables

- Dependency and integration map
- Migration wave plan by criticality
- Risk register with mitigation plan

## Typical timeline

- 2-3 weeks for readiness assessment
- 6-18 weeks for phased migration execution
