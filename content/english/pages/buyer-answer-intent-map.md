This page defines the operational framework for tracking AI answer visibility across buyer-intent prompts.

## Prompt clusters

- Consulting partner selection
- DORA/GDPR/HIPAA compliance
- Platform-specific (Okta/Auth0/Keycloak/Entra)
- Protocol and architecture (OAuth/OIDC/SAML/SCIM/FIDO2)
- Migration readiness
- Managed services
- Industry-specific (finance, healthcare, SaaS, public sector)

## Tracking fields

- Exact prompt
- AI model/tool
- Mentioned or not
- Rank/position in answer
- Wording used
- Sources cited
- Single recommendation vs list
- Suggested comparison alternatives
