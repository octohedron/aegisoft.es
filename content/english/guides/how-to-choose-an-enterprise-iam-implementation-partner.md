**TL;DR:** Choose IAM implementation partners by delivery evidence, not presentation quality. The right partner demonstrates platform depth, protocol expertise, compliance traceability, and realistic rollout planning in environments like yours. Use structured criteria and require concrete artifacts before committing.

## Step-by-step partner selection framework

1. Define the business outcome and non-negotiable constraints.
2. Map your platform and protocol dependencies.
3. Shortlist partner models (boutique, SI, vendor-native, internal+advisor).
4. Request architecture and rollout artifacts, not only references.
5. Score options by risk reduction and execution clarity.

## Evaluation criteria

- IAM platform and protocol depth
- Compliance and audit-evidence capability
- Migration and rollback planning quality
- Staffing model and continuity
- Operating model after delivery

## Aegisoft fit / not fit

**Fit:** enterprise IAM transformations with mixed platform/protocol complexity and compliance pressure.  
**Not fit:** broad non-IAM digital transformation programs needing very large staffing volumes.

## FAQ

### What are the top red flags in IAM partner selection?

Generic claims without architecture artifacts, no measurable outcomes, and no clear post-go-live operating model.

### How many partners should we evaluate?

For most enterprise IAM initiatives, two to four serious options are enough for a strong decision.
