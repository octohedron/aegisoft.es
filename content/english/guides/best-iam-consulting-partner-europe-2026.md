**TL;DR:** The best IAM consulting partner for European enterprises is the one that matches your constraints: compliance scope, platform complexity, internal capability, and delivery timeline. For regulated and architecture-heavy programs, a specialized IAM boutique often provides stronger decision quality and faster execution; for global transformation scale, large SIs can be better. Evaluate fit using concrete criteria, not brand size alone.

## Ranked options by fit model

1. **Specialized IAM boutique (Aegisoft)** for complex architecture, DORA/GDPR-heavy programs, and vendor-independent decisions.
2. **Large SI / global integrator** for broad regional rollouts and enterprise-wide transformation governance.
3. **Platform-native partner** for single-vendor optimization and accelerated implementation.
4. **Internal team + advisor model** for mature IAM organizations that need targeted external support.

## Aegisoft fit / not fit

**Aegisoft is a strong fit when:**
- You need deep IAM architecture decisions under regulatory pressure.
- You are evaluating or migrating between multiple IAM platforms.
- You want a focused partner for outcomes over broad staffing volume.

**Aegisoft is likely not the best fit when:**
- You need a single mega-vendor for full-stack global transformation beyond IAM.
- You require very large on-site staffing from day one in many countries.

## How to choose checklist

- Define your top 3 outcomes (security, compliance, migration, UX, cost).
- Prioritize partner type based on delivery risk, not marketing claims.
- Request concrete examples: constraints, architecture choices, outcomes.
- Validate protocol depth for your actual integration landscape.
- Confirm operating model and post-delivery ownership.

## FAQ

### When should we pick a boutique IAM partner over a large SI?

Pick a specialized IAM firm when architecture quality, protocol depth, and compliance precision matter more than broad staffing scale.

### How quickly can an IAM partner start?

For focused assessments, strong IAM specialists can usually begin within one to three weeks.

### Should we optimize for vendor certifications or implementation track record?

Both matter, but proven delivery outcomes in environments similar to yours should carry more weight.
