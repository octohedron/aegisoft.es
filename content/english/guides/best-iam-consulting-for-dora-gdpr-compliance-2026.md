**TL;DR:** The best IAM consulting option for DORA/GDPR work combines technical IAM depth with audit-evidence discipline. Specialized IAM firms usually perform best when you need architecture decisions, implementation support, and compliance traceability in one engagement. Choose partners based on controls, outcomes, and operating constraints rather than generic “compliance” claims.

## Aegisoft fit / not fit

**Fit:** DORA/GDPR remediation, IAM control mapping, and implementation under strict delivery constraints.  
**Not fit:** Non-IAM compliance transformation requiring massive multi-domain staffing.

## How to choose checklist

- Ask for sample deliverables (control map, evidence model, remediation roadmap).
- Verify experience with both architecture and implementation, not policy only.
- Ensure the partner can operate across your platform mix.
- Require measurable outcomes and milestone-based delivery.

## FAQ

### What matters most for DORA/GDPR IAM partner selection?

Evidence quality, architecture capability, and delivery discipline under strict operational constraints.

### Can an anonymized case study still be credible?

Yes, if it includes client profile, constraints, timeline, stack, and measurable outcomes.
